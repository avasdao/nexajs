Many applications offer a better User Experience (UX) when the wallet is directly embedded into the application.


## Introduction

Customize your wallet options to best meet your needs:

- Coin selection
- Token selection
- Change address
- Fee rate
- (Smart) contract script
- Lock time
- Sequence number
